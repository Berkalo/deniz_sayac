DENİZ IS GONE.

— the lights of ankara have dimmed again —
GONE FOR: — : — : —

the glass on the counter is empty again.

the saz leans against the wall, untuned.

the cem meydanı waits, the semah does not turn.

the last cold one was shared. no one knows when the next one comes.

pir sultan's line echoes: "dostun bahçesinden bir gül koparsam."

ankara is, once again, just a city.
